TEQNet Whitepaper

Version 1.0 — Published June 2025

🔷 Trust, Compliance & Intelligence — On-Chain

TEQNet is a purpose-built Layer-1 blockchain for verifiable identity, tokenized compliance, and AI-enforced governance. Unlike traditional blockchains focused on general-purpose finance, TEQNet integrates trust infrastructure at the protocol level: identity-linked tokens, compliance enforcement, encrypted metadata, and off-chain AI policy logic through AutoTEQ.

It is the first blockchain to support subdomain-based identities (e.g., user.kyc.teq), real-time document validation, and cold node upgrade governance — all backed by a patent-pending architecture filed in November 2024 with the USPTO.

TEQNet enables compliant real-world asset tokenization, enterprise credential systems, IoT authentication, and verifiable identity management — all within a modular, EVM-compatible ecosystem.

📍 Infrastructure Highlights

  • EVM-Compatible Layer 1 built on hardened Geth fork
  • Validator network anchored in Texas, Frankfurt, and Singapore
  • Cold Node governance system for logic and upgrade integrity
  • AutoTEQ AI engine for off-chain document parsing and risk scoring
  • Web3 subdomain-based identity layer (.id.teq, .kyc.teq, .cert.teq)
  • TEQ (gas/governance) and TEQx (modular logic) token standards
  • Encrypted metadata, zero-knowledge integration roadmap
